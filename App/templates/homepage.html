About Us

Welcome to Cram Review! We are Sam and Matt, dedicated to bridging a crucial gap in learning. Our mission is to empower users by offering a comprehensive platform that seamlessly transforms PDFs, videos, and other content into interactive flashcards.


Recognizing the growing importance of artificial intelligence (AI) in education, we saw an opportunity to enhance the learning experience with our innovative product, Cram Review. Our platform not only facilitates flashcard creation but also integrates cutting-edge AI resources, augmenting the learning journey beyond conventional methods.


At Cram Review, we are committed to facilitating effective, personalized learning through technology. Join us as we redefine the way knowledge is accessed and retained.
